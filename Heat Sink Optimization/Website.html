Defining an Optimization Problem

nTopology can calculate total surface area (A) and nodal heat flux (qn)

Heat flow rate is the product of area and flux

Two-objective optimization

of a large, unknown search space NSGA-II

(Non-dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm II)

Objective Functions:

Decision Variables:

Lattice 2 type (Gyroid, Schwarz, Diamond, Lidinoid , SplitP, Neovious) [16]

Mix Factor [0,1]

Thickness [.2mm, .5mm]
